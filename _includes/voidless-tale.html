About the Game

Voidless Tale is a side-scrolling action RPG where every domain has two reflections: celestial and infernal. Track down memory scraps, craft keys to open domain gates, and confront each sin’s guardian in both its Heaven and Hell forms. After every fight, decide between classic loot or absorbing the boss’s signature skill into a Veinshard—assemble a bespoke kit and swap between up to three absorbed abilities on the fly. Climb from level 1 to 1000, unlock a late-game Demigod state, and choose the fate of both realms.

Key Features

Mirrored Domains: Heaven & Hell variants for each sin (SALIGIA), with distinct spaces, enemies, and bosses.
Loot or Absorb: Equip drops or imprint enemy skills into Veinshards, then quick-swap and chain them in combat.
Deep Progression: Level 1–1000 with stat allocation (STR, AGI, INT, VIT, LUCK) and build-defining milestones.
Crafting & Keys: Memory scraps → recipes → materials → crafted keys → boss domains and endgame “Sinful Duality”.
Mod-ready: JSON-style configs, in-game loader, and the standalone Voidless Smith tool for weapon sprites/data.

World & Story

You wake beneath the World Tree—Purgatory’s quiet heart. Scattered memory scraps expose your life’s transgressions: pride, greed, lust, envy, gluttony, wrath, and sloth. Each sin opens a gate with a radiant (Heaven) and profane (Hell) reflection. Defeat both to unlock Sinful Duality, the endgame realm where you decide: rule Heaven, rule Hell, or break the cycle and escape.

Combat & Builds

Responsive 2D combat with melee chains, charged strikes, and ranged skills learned from foes. Absorb abilities into Veinshards, swap them mid-fight, and build synergies via essences, cooldown tweaks, and stat choices. Late-game Demigod form changes how you traverse and fight.
